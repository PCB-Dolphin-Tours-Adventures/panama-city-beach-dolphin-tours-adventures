Frequently Asked Questions

Do wild dolphins like humans?

Dolphins are often regarded as friendly animals, but in reality they are wild animals that should be treated with respect and caution. Dolphins' behavior changes when they interact with humans. The dolphins lose their natural fear, making them vulnerable to vandalism or shark attacks.

What is the best time for dolphin tours?

It is recommended that you go to a dolphin tour in the morning or at noon when there are fewer boats and the water is calmest. The temperature and wind increase in the afternoon. A calmer water surface allows for improved visibility.

Where do dolphins love?

Ocean Dolphins are found in all oceans, seas and rivers. Some dolphins prefer coastal areas while others live in shallower water further out at sea. Some species travel to the high seas, far from land.

What do you wear to a dolphin encounter?

After selecting your dolphin swim experience you will need to wear comfortable clothing as well as a swimsuit. For women, we recommend full-body suits and for men, shorts. You should also avoid wearing necklaces, bracelets and watches.
